Why is This Needed?

As scholars we collate and produce data a vast amount of data in the course of our research. Academic publications, quite rightly, require that data be made available as part of the publication process in order to enable other scholars to validate results by enabling reproducibility. In the Internet age, this data has entirely new potential: as an input into new research -- for example, modern biomedical science makes universal use of digital data.
